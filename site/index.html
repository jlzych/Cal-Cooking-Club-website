Second General Meeting

Tuesday, Nov. 16th from 7:30 - 8:30 PM at KDR

This is just a reminder for our Second General Meeting tomorrow, November 16th from 7:30 - 8:30 PM at KDR (2739 Channing Circle).

Vanessa from Vanessa's Bistro will be demonstrating Vietnamese style shrimp (and tofu) rolls and curry chicken!

So come with an appetite (since there will be plentiful samples), make sure you sign up for our events, and pick up/buy our CCC t-shirts!

The Culinary Arts at CAL

Cooking Club is a great way for students to take advantage of the extraordinary food culture of the Bay Area. We engage a diverse palate of eaters of all ages- from entering freshman to graduate students, novice cooks to seasoned chefs, let Cal Cooking Club be your guide to the gastronomic delights of Berkeley!
