Dine About Town co-coordinator - Christine Nguyen

  • Interests My interests vary a lot. They include: art, food, books/reading, photography, writing, music, traveling, eating, health/fitness, and more!
  • What i do I am one of the coordinators for Dine About Town. What the Dine About Town coordinator does is organize nights when members can gather and collectively go to eat out at some restaurant around Berkeley (and maybe even San Francisco). As a Dine about Town coordinator, I, along with my co-coordinator, Fritz Foo, choose the restaurant and talk to the restaurant management in order to arrange the event for Cal Cooking Club members. This year, both Fritz and I are hoping to take club members to restaurants that Cal Students wouldn't normally eat at (either they're out of the way, hole-in-the-wall type places, or nice restaurants that one wouldn't eat at unless one had an occasion to). Our goal is to expose Berkeley students to more dining options and create more social events for members to attend. Personally, I'm hoping to be able to take cooking club members to some fabulous restaurants this semester as well as plan some Snack about Town events as well.
