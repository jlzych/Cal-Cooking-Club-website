Upcoming Events

General Meetings

Three times a semester Cooking Club hosts general meetings that are free to students and open to the public. At these meetings chefs from much-loved local restaurants such as La Mediterranee, Venezia, Corso Trattoria, and Baywolf give delicious demos of a variety of cuisines. Come for plenty of free samples, to learn more about what we do, and to sign up for events!

Restaurant Sessions

We send you into the kitchens of local restaurants to cook with the chef! A one-of-a-kind opportunity as well as an affordable professional cooking lesson.

Student Cooking Workshops

Get a lesson from a more experienced member of the club. An informal style cooking lesson that is sure to leave you with a wonderful recipe to take home!

Dine About Town

Explore new restaurants with fellow club members and write reviews for Yelp -CAL!

Potlucks

Entertaining and delicious themes! Prizes awarded for best dish.

Community Outreach

Discover ways in which food and cooking can benefit others.

Snack Stop
